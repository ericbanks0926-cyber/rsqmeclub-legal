Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

RSQMECLUB.COM does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information beyond what is reasonably necessary to provide the Services or comply with applicable law. Payment card information is processed by Stripe or another authorized payment processor and is not stored by the Company except for limited transaction metadata.

How We Use Personal Information

  • Create and administer Member and Service Provider accounts.
  • Process Membership enrollments, renewals, and payments.
  • Dispatch roadside assistance Service Providers.
  • Communicate regarding service requests and account activity.
  • Verify identity and prevent fraud.
  • Improve the Application, website, and customer experience.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, tax, and contractual obligations.

Location Information

RSQMECLUB.COM uses precise and approximate location information to identify the Member's location, dispatch the nearest available Provider, calculate estimated arrival times, verify completed services, investigate fraud, and improve operational efficiency. Location services may be required for certain features of the Application. Disabling location permissions may limit or prevent use of roadside assistance services.

Payment Processing

Membership fees and approved service charges are processed through Stripe or another authorized payment processor. RSQMECLUB.COM does not store complete payment card numbers. Limited transaction information may be retained for accounting, customer support, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

  • Government authorities when required by law or legal process.
  • A successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.
  • RSQMECLUB.COM does not sell personal information and does not share personal information for targeted advertising as defined under applicable privacy laws.
